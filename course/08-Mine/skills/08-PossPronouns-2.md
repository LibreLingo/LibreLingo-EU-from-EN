##  Possessive Pronouns (Mine)

#### Possessive Pronouns with a plural object
* Example: Motxila gorriak nireak dira.: The red backpacks are mine.
* Note that possessive pronouns take endings (-a, -ak) like nouns and adjectives.
    * nireak: mine
    * zureak: yours
    * bereak: his/hers/its
    * gureak: ours
    * zuenak: yours (plural):
    * haienak: theirs:
