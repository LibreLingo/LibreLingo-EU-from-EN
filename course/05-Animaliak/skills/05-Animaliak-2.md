## Basque names of common animals
*   The Basque region is largely agriculture, so you are going to see animals.

### Vocabulary Animaliak 2
* ahuntz: goat
* ardi: sheep
* arrain: fish
* behi: cow
* sagu: mouse
* suge: snake
* txerri: pig
* zaldi: horse
* marroi - brown
