# 44. Nahi Izan 2
### “Nahi Izan” in the Present Tense.
* In the present form, the dictionary form of the verb is used (not “-ten”), with “Nahi” (no “Izan”) and the present form of the verb to have.  (Ukan)

###  Nahi Izan 2
* nahi: want
* tomate: tomato
* pizza: pizza
* arraultz: egg
* azenario: carrot
* ogitarteko: sandwich
* tipula: onion
